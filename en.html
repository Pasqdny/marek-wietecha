About Me

Engineering precision, hands-on expertise, and relentless focus on quality standards

Technical Precision Across Every Inspection Phase

Hands-on professional with over 13 years of practical experience in Quality Control (QC) and more than 20 years within industrial and manufacturing environments. My primary focus is on advanced industrial metrology, 3D tactile and optical measurements, and comprehensive component inspection against engineering blueprints.

I specialize in inspecting safety-critical components for the Automotive sector, agricultural machinery, welded heavy structures, and precision machinery. Fully versed in IATF 16949 and ISO 9001 standards, I bridge shop-floor manufacturing practices with strict Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) analysis.

Key Areas of Expertise

  • 3D Metrology: Hexagon Absolute Arms, 3D scanning, laser inspection, optical metrology.
  • Inspection Software: PolyWorks Inspector, PC-DMIS, Geomagic Control X.
  • Supplier Quality Inspection: Incoming goods verification, initial sample inspection (FAI), product audits.
  • Blueprint Reading: Engineering drawing interpretation, GD&T / ISO GPS tolerancing.
  • Quality Methods: 8D problem solving, 5Why, Ishikawa, FMEA, PPAP/APQP compliance.
