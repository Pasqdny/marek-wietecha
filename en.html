About Me

Passion for precision, practical technical expertise, and continuous focus on quality

Engineering Precision at Every Inspection Stage

A practical expert with over 13 years of professional experience in Quality Control (QC) and more than 20 years in industrial and manufacturing environments. My competencies focus on advanced industrial metrology, tactile and optical 3D measurements, and comprehensive verification of part compliance against engineering drawings.

I specialize in working with critical components for the Automotive sector, agricultural machinery, welded structures, and heavy industry. I have deep knowledge of IATF 16949 and ISO 9001 quality systems, combining manufacturing process insight with a rigorous approach to Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T).

Key Specialization Areas

  • 3D Measurement: Hexagon Absolute Arm articulated arms, 3D scanning, optical metrology.
  • Metrology Software: PolyWorks Inspector, PC-DMIS, Geomagic Control X.
  • Supplier Quality Management: Incoming part inspection, product audits, FAI verification.
  • Drawing Analysis: Engineering drawing interpretation, GD&T / ISO GPS tolerancing.
  • Quality Tools: 8D, 5Why, Ishikawa, FMEA, PPAP/APQP control, non-conformance management.
