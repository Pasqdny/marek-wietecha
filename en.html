Executive Summary

I am a Senior Quality Engineer and Industrial Metrologist specializing in high-precision measurement, supplier quality assurance, and quality system management for demanding automotive and machinery industries.

My expertise spans from shop-floor CMM programming and 3D optical scanning to complex GD&T blueprint validation and root-cause non-conformity investigations using 8D, 5Why, and Ishikawa methods.

Core Philosophy

Quality engineering is not merely about identifying non-conformities; it is about building reliable, repeatable processes that prevent defects at the source.

By combining metrological rigor with data analytics (SPC, MSA) and ERP integrations (SAP QM, Siemens Teamcenter), I ensure seamless cross-functional execution between Engineering, Production, and Suppliers.
